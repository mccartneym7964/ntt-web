Sustainability

⚡NTT Pharmacuetical's facilities are powered 100% by renewable energy. We strive to make our operations as efficient as possible and minimize waste.

🌳We have ample green-space near all of our facilities that employees and the community at large can enjoy.

♻️We are working with our partners to develop a closed-loop recycling system, where minimal waste will be generated throughout the supply chain.
